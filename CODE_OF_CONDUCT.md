# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in NeuralInverse CE a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our standards

Positive behavior includes:
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Accepting feedback gracefully

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or personal attacks of any kind
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to github@neuralinverse.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
